Annadhanam: Donate Food & Support Daily Temple Meals

The Annadhanam is considered the highest form of charity, enshrined in the age-old promise that no devotee or poor person need ever leave the temple on an empty stomach. Your donation today becomes someone's meal, someone's blessing. Donate food to the poor and support temple food donation programs that serve fresh, wholesome meals every day.

The need for Annadhanam is urgent now. With escalating costs of food and growing numbers of daily devotees, temple kitchens feel the mounting pressure. Many underprivileged people survive on these daily meals, so continued support becomes necessary. By donating, you help feed the poor, further strengthen temple food donation efforts, and support a sacred tradition that feeds into the lives of many.

Support Daily Meals

Why Donation is Urgent

Annadhanam support has become an urgent need. Every day, more and more devotees and poor families come to the temple, and these meals are often their only source of nourishment. As the footfall increases with limited resources, so does the demand, and the number of regular sponsors remains few. In this situation, even a small step in donating a meal goes a long way.

With the increase in food prices, temple kitchens bear an additional burden of sustaining every single day's meals uninterruptedly. People who come here do not have any other place to go; therefore, this charity venture in food is a lifeline. Annadhanam is the quickest, easiest way to make a real difference because it's direct service: your donation becomes food on a person's plate.

Sponsorship Options

Daily Annadhanam Sponsorship

Sponsor a day's afternoon meal only and make a real difference in people's lives. By choosing the "Donate for Food" option, you can provide hygienic, nutritious, and freshly prepared temple meals to 100 people. Daily Annadhanam is a simple and direct way to help, as many rely on these temple meals every day

Festival Annadhanam Sponsorship

During festivals, the number of devotees and needy families increases multifold. Your contribution to Temple Food Donation or food donation programs covers the high volume of festival day feeding so that everyone can enjoy the celebration without hunger. Festival Annadhanam is an effective form of food charity that touches hundreds of lives at a time.

Monthly / Annual Donor Plans

Our Annadhanam Donation Online plans offer a sustainable way for families and CSR donors to support temple meals throughout the year. Monthly or annual sponsorship lets you regularly feed devotees and underprivileged families, maintaining continuity in the sacred tradition of daily Annadhanam.
