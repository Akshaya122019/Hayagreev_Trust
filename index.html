Our Vision & Mission

Vision:

We dream of a world where every child can learn without worry, every family has enough to eat, and every couple can celebrate their sacred union with dignity and joy. Our vision at Hayagreev is to nurture communities that are caring, compassionate, and connected, with generosity and empathy being at the very heart of everyday life.

Mission:

Our mission is to empower underprivileged people by providing nourishing meals through Annadhanam, essential educational materials to children, and support for temple weddings for poor couples. As a transparent public charitable trust, we ensure that every donation we get delivers a meaningful, lasting difference.

Every gift you give spreads hope, strengthens communities, and multiplies blessings.

About Hayagreev Charity Foundation

With empathy and a commitment to service, Hayagreev works to serve the needs of needy people through education, food, and sacred weddings. By choosing to support a charity, you can bring hope, nourishment, and education to people who need it most, making a real difference in their lives. As a registered public charity organisation, our goal is to effect lasting change in human lives:

*

Providing daily meals to people dependent on temple kitchens.

*

Supplying educational resources that inspire children to dream big and reach their potential.

*

Supporting temple weddings for poor couples, keeping the sacred rituals and tradition going.

Every act of giving, when you donate for charity, becomes a blessing, spreading joy, opportunity, and dignity to underprivileged children and people.

Ways We Serve the Community

Annadhanam

Daily Meals for People

Your charity donation through Annadhanam helps bring warm, healthy food onto the plates of devotees and poorer people every day. For many, this is their only meal, so kindly donate to the poor and count your blessings.

Why Your Help Matters:

With rising food costs and increasing numbers of daily visitors, temple kitchens are facing enormous challenges. Your donation ensures no one leaves hungry and that this sacred tradition of feeding others continues uninterrupted.

Sponsorship Options:
  • Daily Annadhanam: Feeding 100 people daily with freshly prepared meals.
  • Festival Annadhanam: Support large festival-day meals, reaching hundreds of people.
  • Monthly / Annual Plans: Ensure ongoing support to continue this crucial program.
Every meal you share becomes nourishment for both body and soul.
Temple Marriage

Support - Bless Poor Couples

Many poor couples cannot afford even the basic necessities of a wedding and are unable to experience the blessings of a holy wedding. Hayagreev steps in to support a charity, their celebration of temple marriages with dignity. Donate for charity and help with wedding rituals.

Sponsorship Packages:
  • Full Temple Marriage Sponsorship: Covers hall, priest, rituals, garlands, and basic feast.
  • Partial Sponsorship: Sponsor specific parts of the ceremony, such as rituals, priests, garlands, or feasts.
Each gift preserves tradition, spreads joy, and blesses lives in ways that would last an eternity.
Education Support

Donate Notebooks & Books

Every child should have the opportunity to learn, dream, and grow. However, even today many students lack basic learning tools. Your contribution to our public charitable trust provides notebooks, pens, books, and learning materials that enable children to pursue education with confidence and dignity.

Donation Options:
  • Notebook Packs: Support n number of students.
  • Book Donation Packs: Donate textbooks, storybooks, moral books, and learning aids.
  • Full School Supplies Kit: Notebooks, pens, pencils, rulers, and erasers for a full year of learning.
Everything you give plants the seeds of knowledge, confidence, and opportunity.
