Support Poor Couples’ Wedding

Why Your Donation Matters

Every year, countless underprivileged couples struggle to gather the essentials for a wedding. Rising costs of ceremonies prevent many from participating in traditional rituals, leaving them without the blessings of their families and community.

By offering a Temple Wedding Donation through Kalyana Udavi, you can remove these financial barriers and allow couples to celebrate with dignity. Your generosity ensures that even those with limited means can experience a charity marriage that honors both spiritual and cultural traditions.

Donation Process

Supporting a temple marriage through Kalyana Udavi is easy, safe, and transparent. Your Temple Wedding Donation brings joy, dignity, and blessings to underprivileged couples, giving them a sacred start to their married life.

Online Donation via Debit Card

Donate using any major debit card. The process is secure and simple—just enter your card details, confirm, and your contribution is done. You will immediately receive confirmation and updates about the wedding arrangements.

Online Donation via Credit Card

You can also contribute via credit card. This is convenient for donors who prefer to pay through their credit account. Every rupee goes directly toward the wedding ceremony, with transparent tracking.

Net Banking

Donors can transfer directly from their bank account using net banking. This method is safe, fast, and ensures your donation reaches the couple without delay. Confirmation and updates are sent to you after the transaction.

UPI And QR Scans

For quick and hassle-free donations, you can also use UPI apps or scan QR codes, which allow instant and secure transfers. Supported options include popular platforms such as GPay (Google Pay), PhonePe, and PayPal and others. This method ensures that your Temple Wedding Donation reaches the couple immediately, making it simple for anyone to contribute and bring blessings, joy, and dignity to underprivileged couples.

Donor Reassurance

After donating, you will receive:

  • Acknowledgement of your contribution via email or SMS
  • Verified updates on how your donation is helping the couple
  • Assurance that 100% of your contribution goes directly to the temple wedding

Your generosity is more than financial support—it is a life-changing gift that provides couples with blessings, joy, and a dignified start to their life together.
